## Description
DahoHelping is a web-based forum conceptualized for Vietnam National University - Ho Chi Minh City (VNU-HCM). Developed in 2024 by a team of IT students from the University of Information Technology, it serves as a trusted platform for students across the VNU system to ask questions, share knowledge, and support each other in academics and daily life. With a simple, user-friendly interface, rich features, and high security, DahoHelping aims to become the leading student forum in Ho Chi Minh City.
